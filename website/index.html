Summary

I am a Doctor Emeritus in MCB (Multiple Composition of Bangers), as part of the Cisco School of Cool Stuff. I teach and carry out research in a variety of topics that all classify as Cool Stuff. As a result, I am able to know a lot about a multitude of subjects, which gives me the legitimacy to mansplain my way through life, albeit not having strong opinion on anything.
